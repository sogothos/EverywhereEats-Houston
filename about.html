We are located in Houston, TX in Zone 9a of the USDA Hardiness Zones. Our climate allows us to grow native produce as well as those from semi-tropical climates. Because of Houston's diverse population, we provide plenty of cultural produce to the local Asian and African community.

We provide produce to local HISD public schools, local restaurants (ranging from fast casual to fine dining), and ... You can buy our produce at the local Farmer's Market every Friday - Saturday, in the special section at select H-E-B grocery stores, or from our online store.

Everywhere Eats is sustained by our wonderful team of staff and volunteers. To join our team, please fill out this form. We are not only in search of people with an agricultural background! We require the talents of many disciplines such as engineers to enhance our hydroponic system, financiers to help manage our assets, or journalists to increase our public image and outreach. Anyone is helpful!
